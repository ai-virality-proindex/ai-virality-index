# Case Study #1: ChatGPT Agent Launch — The Biggest Spike in Index History

## The Event
On February 19, 2026, OpenAI launched ChatGPT Agent — evolving Operator into a unified agentic system with 256K context window, shopping features, and autonomous task execution. It rolled out to Pro users first, with Plus and Team access following.

## What AVI Showed

### Before (Feb 18)
- ChatGPT VI_trade: **44.75**
- All components at moderate levels

### The Spike (Feb 19)
- ChatGPT VI_trade: **98.69** (+54 points in 24 hours)
- **Every single component maxed out simultaneously:**
  - T (Trends): 92.7 — Google search interest exploded
  - S (Social): 100 — YouTube and HN discussion saturated
  - G (GitHub): 100 — Developer repos surged
  - N (News): 100 — Global news coverage wall-to-wall
  - D (DevAdoption): 100 — npm/PyPI SDK downloads spiked
  - M (Mindshare): 100 — Wikipedia pageviews peaked

### The Decay Pattern
| Day | VI_trade | Decline |
|-----|----------|---------|
| Feb 19 | 98.69 | Peak |
| Feb 20 | ~90 | -9% |
| Feb 21 | ~89 | -1% |
| Feb 22 | ~83 | -7% |
| Feb 23 | ~55 | -34% (cliff) |
| Feb 28 | ~41 | Normalized |

**Decay half-life: 3-4 days** — consistent with major product launches.

### Cross-Model Impact (Zero-Sum Effect)
When ChatGPT spiked to 98.69, ALL other models dropped sharply:
- Claude: 55.25 → 38.50 (-30%)
- Gemini: 48.25 → 20.56 (-57%)
- DeepSeek: 48.25 → 25.00 (-48%)

This confirms zero-sum dynamics in attention markets: when one model captures all attention, competitors are crowded out.

## Trading Signal
If a trader saw the spike on Feb 19 with ALL 6 components at 100, the correct interpretation:
1. **Day 0-2**: Ride the momentum — adoption metrics (D=100) confirm the hype is real
2. **Day 3+**: Begin exiting — historical pattern shows 3-4 day half-life
3. **Day 7+**: Look for mean reversion in competitors (Claude, Gemini were oversold)

## Key Takeaway
> **AVI captured the single biggest AI product launch of Q1 2026 with same-day precision.** The 6-component breakdown provided actionable context: when ALL components max out simultaneously, it signals a genuine market-moving event — not noise. The decay pattern is predictable and tradeable.
